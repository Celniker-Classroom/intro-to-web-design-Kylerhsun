Who is Kyler Sun?

Kyler Sun was born on September 17, 2009 to Sharon Chen and Eric Sun. Kyler has two parents and a younger sister, and many cousins both in China and in the U.S. He is currently a high school student at Canyon Crest Academy in San Diego, California. At a young age he participated in many extracurricular activities including swimming, Kung Fu, Piano, etc. He eventually stuck with two events, basketball and clarinet.

Kyler`s Basketball Journey

Starting at a very young age Kyler started playing basketball, and although it wasn`t very serious at first it fostered an enjoyment towards basketball. He was naturally very fast, and could jump higher than others, which led him to be able to score as many points as he wanted with just his right hand layup. Only when he fractured his right hand was he able to learn how to consistently make left handed layups almost as skilfully as his right handed layups. Kyler ignored important skills because at the time there was no need to develop them, as his physical abilities were enough. As everyone else grew taller, he started to fall behind, which caused him to seriously start focusing on the core skills of basketball. Unfortunately as of now, he is out due to an ACL tear.
